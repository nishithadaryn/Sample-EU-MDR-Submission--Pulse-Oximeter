# Sample EU MDR Submission – Pulse Oximeter

## Overview

This repository contains a sample EU MDR regulatory documentation package developed for a Pulse Oximeter as a self-directed Regulatory Affairs portfolio project. The objective of this project was to gain practical experience with the documentation, risk management, clinical evaluation, and post-market activities required throughout the medical device lifecycle under Regulation (EU) 2017/745 (EU MDR).

The project simulates the core documentation that may be prepared during the conformity assessment process for a medical device and demonstrates an understanding of how clinical evidence, risk management, technical documentation, and post-market surveillance activities are integrated to support regulatory compliance.

---

## Documents Included

- Technical Documentation
- Clinical Evaluation Report (CER)
- Risk Management File
- Declaration of Conformity

The documentation covers device description, intended purpose, clinical background, literature review, clinical data appraisal, safety analysis, benefit-risk evaluation, hazard analysis, Failure Mode and Effects Analysis (FMEA), risk control measures, labeling, Instructions for Use (IFU), Post-Market Surveillance (PMS) planning, and Post-Market Clinical Follow-Up (PMCF) planning.

---

## Regulatory Frameworks Referenced

- Regulation (EU) 2017/745 (EU MDR)
- ISO 14971:2019 – Medical Devices – Application of Risk Management to Medical Devices
- ISO 13485:2016 – Medical Devices – Quality Management Systems
- IEC 62304 – Medical Device Software Lifecycle Processes
- IEC 62366-1 – Usability Engineering for Medical Devices
- IEC 60601-1 – Medical Electrical Equipment Safety
- IEC 60601-1-2 – Electromagnetic Compatibility (EMC)
